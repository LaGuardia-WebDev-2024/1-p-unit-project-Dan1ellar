Hunger Games Districts

District 1, Luxury💎

  • One of the wealthiest districts
  • Produces items like jewlery
  • Characters: Marvel, Gloss, Cashmere, Glimmer

District 2, Masonry🧱

  • Located in the Rocky Mountains
  • Known for it's weapon manufacturing
  • Characters: Cato, Clove

District 3,Technology⚙

  • Produces electronics, televisions, and computers
  • Located in the east and spans across Illinois, Minnesota, Iowa, and Indiana
  • Characters: Beetee

District 4, Fishing🐟

  • Located on the west coast in Nevada and California
  • Produces fish products
  • Characters: Finnick, Annie, Mags

District 5, Power⚡

  • Produces electricity
  • Located on the west coast
  • Characters: Foxface

District 6, Transportation🚝

  • Located in the midwest in states like Michigan and Wisconsin
  • Produces vehicles and other parts
  • Characters: N/A

District 7, Lumber🪓

  • Located in Oregon, Washington, and some of southwestern Canada
  • Produces wood products
  • Characters: Johanna, Lamina, Treech

District 8, Textiles🧵

  • Produces fabrics and is known for making peacekeeper uniforms
  • One of the poorest districts
  • Characters: Bobbin, Wovey

District 9, Grain 🌾

  • One of the largest districts
  • Produces crops like barley, corn, oat, and rice
  • Characters: N/A

District 10, Livestock 🐮

  • Produces all animal products including meat, eggs, hides, feathers, and honey
  • Located on the Southern border
  • Characters: N/A

District 11, Agriculture 🌽

  • Produces things like fruits and vegetables
  • One of the poorest districts
  • Characters: Rue, Thresh

District 12, Mining⛏

  • Located in Kentucky, West Virginia, and Pennsylvania
  • Produces mostly coal
  • Characters: Katniss, Peeta, Haymitch, Lucy Gray, etc.

Honorable Mention: The Capitol 👑

  • The wealthiest of all of Panem
  • Characters: Snow, Effie, Cressida, etc.
